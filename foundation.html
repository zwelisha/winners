ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

Overview

Winner`s life coach foundation is a non profit organisation focused on creating winning communities either in the secular or religious spheres of life, winning civil societies, winning co-operate society, winning global citizens and to turn individuals in to agents of change by training them to become winners in their respective areas of personal developments; At its micro level the Winner`s life coach foundation seeks to program individuals for success and fulfilment of their human potential trough varying life coaching strategies and methods. At its macro level the winner`s foundation seeks to transform communities and the world in all its multidimensional configurations. The winner`s life coach foundation was founded by Mr Khaya Mayile in 2014 who is currently presiding as a Founding Director of the foundation. The winner`s life coach foundation as an idea is an intellectual property which legally belongs to the Director of the Foundation

Vision

premised from its logo, “ Losers accept what they can’t change, winners change what they can’t accept” the Vision of the Winner`s Life Coach Foundation is to serve as an agent of change with the purpose of creating winners at a micro and macro level!

Aims and Objectives

The ultimate aim and objective of the Winner`s life coach foundation is to create winning communities either in the secular or religious spheres of life, winning civil societies, winning co-operate society, winning global citizens and to turn individuals in to agents of change by training them to become winners in their respective areas of personal developments; At its micro level the Winner`s life coach foundation seeks to program individuals for success and fulfilment of their human potential trough varying life coaching strategies and methods. At its macro level the winner`s foundation seeks to transform communities and the world in all its multidimensional configurations
