ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Abridged biography

Khaya Wisdom Mayile was born in George in the Western Cape, he matriculated with flying winning colours on top of his class. He then pursued several degrees in the University of the Western Cape graduating with distinctions while pursuing leadership studies, thus on top of his degrees he achieved further two more qualifications in Leadership skills and advanced leadership skills. While advancing his studies further Mr Khaya Mayile is currently an adhoc associate of John Maxwell`s mentorship training program and has completed a life couch accredited diploma program with a Cape Town based life coaching institution. Khaya Wisdom Mayile was not only in the dean`s merit list which is an official University benchmark for high academic performers, but he is also a winner of several awards including the USA based prestigious Mellon accolade for outstanding academic performance; Khaya has also received a prestigious award for the best essay in an essay writing competition, poetry accolades and research placement accolades. While pursuing his second degree Khaya was awarded an award as a best mentor and on the subsequent year he was picked as the best head-mentor. Khaya Mayile received a first class selection as part of a group of leaders who went to the United States of America in Washington DC and New York to gain further international leadership skills.

Mr Khaya Wisdom Mayile has played the leadership role on many capacities including the ‘All Africa Youth leadership Panel’ led by Former statesman Mr Mvuyelwa Thabo Mbeki. Mr Khaya is frequently requested to serve as leader of different organisations, boards and is currently an adhoc advisor in parliament to a Minister of the ruling party. He is also a public intellectual-academic writing in different platforms including South Africa`s print media especially the NEW AGE, THE STAR and BUSNESS DAY tackling global issues of governance; for a quick post-view of his writings you may see his article which was a critique of the NDP and South Africa`s political pathologies titled “Identifying with the bourgeoisies” published by the New Age on the 14th 2014 April. www.thenewage.co.za On top of many un-mentioned accolades Khaya Wisdom Mayile is a professional motivational speaker, life coach and an author who is passionate about the development of human potential to its fullest and the practical application of wisdom to achieve one`s goals thereby becoming a winner in life.
